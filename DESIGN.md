# Design System Principles: Friendly Modernity

 

This design system is built to balance professional clarity with an approachable, modern personality. By utilizing soft shapes and high-readability typography, we create an interface that feels both cutting-edge and welcoming.

 

## Color Strategy

Our palette is anchored by a vibrant teal primary color, providing a sense of energy and technological precision.

- **Primary Color**: #00B4D8 (Teal) serves as the main driver for interaction and brand identity.

- **Surface & Backgrounds**: A light mode approach using neutral tones (#1A1C1E for high-contrast elements) ensures a clean, airy environment that minimizes cognitive load.

 

## Typography

The typographic scale is designed for distinct hierarchy and effortless reading.

- **Headlines**: Epilogue is used for headings to provide a bold, contemporary character.

- **Body & Labels**: Manrope is utilized for its functional excellence and geometric clarity, ensuring legibility across all screen sizes.

 

## Shape and Structure

The defining characteristic of this updated system is its softness. 

- **Roundedness**: We have moved to a maximum roundedness setting (3). This translates to pill-shaped buttons, fully rounded chips, and soft-cornered containers, reducing the "sharpness" of the UI to make it feel more organic and inviting.

- **Spacing**: We maintain a standard spacing scale (2) to ensure a balanced distribution of elements, providing enough room for components to breathe without sacrificing information density.

 

## Component Behavior

All interactive components leverage the pill-shaped geometry. Buttons, input fields, and tags feature maximum corner radii, creating a consistent visual language that signals a friendly, user-centric experience.
